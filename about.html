Our Goals

The Mercer Vietnamese Student Association is a philanthropic club dedicated to raising funds and increasing awareness for Mercer on Mission's efforts in Vietnam. The club strives to promote diversity around campus by giving students the opportunity to experience foreign culture and connect with their peers. The club hosts monthly student events, like charity dinners & food stalls, for this purpose.

History of the Club

The Mercer Vietnamese Student Association was founded in 2019 by a group of passionate Vietnamese students who wanted to create a vibrant community on campus. What started as a small gathering of friends sharing meals and stories has grown into a thriving organization with over 200 active members.

In its early years, the club focused on cultural celebrations and educational events, organizing traditional Tet Lunar New Year festivities and cooking demonstrations.

As the club matured, members recognized an opportunity to make a meaningful impact beyond campus. In 2021, the VSA partnered with Mercer on Mission to direct fundraising efforts toward projects in Vietnam, including educational scholarships and infrastructure improvements in underserved communities. This pivot transformed the club from a purely social organization into a philanthropic force.

Today, the VSA proudly continues its mission of bridging cultures, fostering community, and creating positive change both on campus and in Vietnam, embodying the values of compassion and service that define the Mercer experience.
